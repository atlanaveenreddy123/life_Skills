### 1. What is Deep Work?

- Deep Work means focusing fully on an important task without distractions.
- It helps you learn faster and produce better results in less time.

### 2. According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?

- Choose one important task to focus on
- Work in a quiet place without distractions
- Take short breaks after deep focus sessions

### 3. How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?

- Plan your most important task for the day first
- Keep your phone away or on silent mode
- After deep work, take a small break to relax your mind.

### 4. What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

- It distracts you from studies and important work
- It can become addictive and hard to stop using
- It can show fake news and negative or misleading information.
