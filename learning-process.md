## 1. What is the Feynman Technique?
- The Feynman Techinque is a learning method where you understand a topic by explaining it in simple words, as if teaching someone else.

## 3. What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
- Active mode of thinking: When you concentrate deeply on learning something specific.
- Diffuse mode of thinking: When your brain is relaxed and helps to connect ideas creatively in the background.

## 4. According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic?
- Break the topic into smaller parts.
- Focus and learn deeply.
- Remove distractions.
- Practice and recall what you learned
