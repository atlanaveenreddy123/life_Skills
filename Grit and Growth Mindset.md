### 1. Paraphrase (summarize) the video of grit?
- Grit means staying focused on your goals even when things get hard.
- This video explains that talent alone is not enough to be successful; effort and long-term commitment also matter.
  
### 2. Paraphrase (summarize) the video of  Growth Mindset.
- The video explains that a growth mindset means believing that we can get better by working hard.
- It also says that mistakes and challenges help us learn and improve.
  
### 3. What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?
- Internal Locus of Control means believing that your success depends on your own actions and hard work.
- The key point of the video is that we stay motivated when we focus on things we can control, instead of blaming others or luck.
